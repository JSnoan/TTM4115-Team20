Exact pixel previews for the Raspberry Pi LED matrix used by DroneClient.py.

TTM4115 Team 20

Runtime mapping

  • docked + battery below 99%: yellow/green lightning bolt, charging.
  • docked + battery at 99% or above: full green battery.
  • navigating: blue/cyan outbound flight arrow.
  • manual_control: orange joystick, operator takeover near destination.
  • waiting_onsite: purple package with green check, arrived onsite.
  • returning: teal/blue dock base, returning to station.
  • low battery or rejected dispatch: white battery outline with red low charge.
  • critical fault or unknown state: red X.
